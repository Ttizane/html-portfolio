The Best Movies According to Riccardo

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Wolf of Wall Street

The movie Leonardo DiCaprio should have gotten an Oscar for. His portrayal of the character is perfect. The movie is well-written, leaving no details out of the original story.

Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas

This seemingly pointless celebration of bad behavior is also a hilarious and crazily visual comedy for adults

Taxi Driver

Quite rightly regarded as one of the best films of all time, TAXI DRIVER is without a doubt compelling viewing. What the film lacks in plot it makes up for in characterisation.
